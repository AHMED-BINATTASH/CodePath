About Dr. Moustafa Saad

He has a PhD in Computer Vision and Deep Learning from Simon Fraser University, with research published in top conferences and over 750 citations. With 9 years of experience in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, he worked on action recognition, pose estimation, and neural networks. He also has 5 years of software engineering experience using Java and C++ in banking and storage systems. In addition, he is active in programming competitions and runs a YouTube channel with 250 videos and 63,000 subscribers, where he teaches algorithms, coding, and problem solving.
